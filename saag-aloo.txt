# Ingredients

- 2 tbsp sunflower oil
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 2 garlic cloves, sliced
- 1 tbsp chopped ginger
- 500g potato, cut into 2cm, 3/4 in chunks
- 1 large red chilli, halved, deseeded and finely sliced
- 1/2 tsp each black mustard seeds, cumin seeds, turmeric
- 250g spinach leaves

# Directions

Heat 2 tbsp sunflower oil in a large pan, add 1 finely chopped onion, 2 sliced garlic cloves and 1 tbsp chopped ginger, and fry for about 3 mins.

Stir in 500g potatoes, cut into 2cm chunks, 1 halved, deseeded and finely sliced red chilli, 1/2 tsp black mustard seeds, 1/2 tsp cumin seeds, 1/2 tsp turmeric and 1/2 tsp salt and continue cooking and stirring for 5 mins more. 

Add a splash of water, cover, and cook for 8-10 mins.

Check the potatoes are ready by spearing with the point of a knife, and if they are, add 250g spinach leaves and let it wilt into the pan.
Take off the heat and serve.
